Our Services

Full Home Interior Design

If you are looking for the best interior designer to build your dream home within your budget, then you should hire “Namis Interior”. We provide end to end interior designing services for your home.

Kitchen and Bathroom Interior Design

We provide complete kitchen and bathroom interior designing services to bring your dream kitchen and bathroom into reality for a sophisticated lifestyle. It includes modular kitchen designing, kitchen remodeling and bathroom renovation.

Full Home Renovation

Are you bored of your existing home interiors and wish to upgrade to the modern trends then, we are here to help you to renovate your home completely. We offer full home makeovers and themed interiors such as contemporary, minimalist, bohemian, or traditional styles according to your wish.

Existing Space Planning & Optimization

At Naims Interior, we take over any size home space and convert it into a most efficient space for your daily life.

Complete Home Customization

At Naims Interior, we offer all types of furniture customization, painting, flooring and plumbing services to bring a new life to your living space and upgrade to the modern trends.

3D Visualization and Online Interior Consultation

If you wish to see your dream home before execution, you can preview it with 3D visualization. We also offer online interior designing consultation service at your convenience.
